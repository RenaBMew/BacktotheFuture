Welcome Back, to the Future.

In this 1980s sci-fi classic, small-town California teen, Marty McFly, gets lost in 1955 by an accident, 30 years back in time. Traveling through time in a modified DeLorean car, Marty encounters young versions of his parents, and must make sure that they fall in love or he'll cease to exist. Even more dauntingly, Marty has to return to his own time and save the life of Doc Brown.
